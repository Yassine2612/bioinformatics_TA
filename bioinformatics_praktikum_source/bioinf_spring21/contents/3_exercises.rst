Learning objectives
-------------------

* You can obtain basic statistics of FASTA-formatted DNA and protein sequence files
* You can formulate a hypothesis regarding the impact of host-pathogen interactions on molecular sequence variations
* You are able to re-arrange a datafile in UNIX or R 
* You analyse and interpret bacterial growth data in R
